Intech Studio's Knot is a USB MIDI Host to TRS MIDI converter solution designed for portability, reliability and ease of use. 

Knot is an OSHWA certified Open-Hardware project! [Certification Page](https://certification.oshwa.org/hu000006.html)

![Photo of the Intech Studio KNOT](https://intech.studio/products/knot/knot_deg_25.svg)
I've been involved with the development of the Intech Studio's Knot since the concept stage in the following areas:

- Electrical Design
- Mechanical Design
- Firmware Development
- Design for Manufacturing

## Tools used
- Mechanical design: FreeCAD
- Electrical design: KiCAD
- Firmware: Written in C (ESP-IDF)
